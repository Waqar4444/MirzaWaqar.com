About the Company

Turner and Hooch Kennel Store is a specialty pet supply shop offering high-quality pet products, accessories, and essentials. With a commitment to pet well-being, the store provides a seamless online shopping experience for pet owners looking for premium products for their furry companions.

Project Objectives

  • User-Friendly Shopify Store: Designed an intuitive and visually appealing eCommerce website.

Solution: Implemented structured product categorization and an efficient filtering system for easy browsing.
